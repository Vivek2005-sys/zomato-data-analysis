# zomato-data-analysis
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Zomato dataset to uncover factors affecting restaurant ratings, customer engagement, and business insights using Python.


## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Zomato dataset to understand factors affecting restaurant ratings, customer engagement, and market trends.

The goal is to extract meaningful insights that can help improve business decisions in the restaurant industry.

## Objectives

- Analyze distribution of restaurant ratings
- Understand impact of pricing on ratings
- Study customer engagement using votes
- Evaluate effect of online delivery on performance
- Identify top cities and cuisines

## Dataset

- Source: Zomato Dataset
- Contains information about restaurants such as:
  - Name, Location, Country
  - Cuisines
  - Average Cost for Two
  - Ratings and Votes
  - Online Delivery and Table Booking

## Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Steps Performed

1. Data Understanding
2. Data Cleaning
3. Feature Engineering
4. Univariate Analysis
5. Bivariate Analysis
6. Multivariate Analysis
7. Key Insights Extraction
8. Business Recommendations

## Key Insights

- Most restaurants have average ratings, indicating high competition
- Pricing does not strongly influence ratings
- Online delivery increases customer engagement
- Few restaurants receive majority of votes
- Dataset is heavily dominated by India

## Business Recommendations

- Focus on customer experience rather than pricing
- Enable online delivery to increase reach
- Encourage customer reviews for better engagement
- Explore niche cuisines for differentiation
